EF Scale:

The Enhanced Fujita scale (originated from Tom Fujita's F-scale) describes the strength of the tornado based on the amount and type of damage caused by the tornado. The F-scale of damage will vary in the destruction area; therefore, the highest value of the F-scale is recorded for each event.
